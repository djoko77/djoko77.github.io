Winter is Here! Try Snowmobiling in Soda Springs!

Necia Seamons

This area has more than 250 miles of snowmobile trails through scenic forested mountains and powdery valley floors. Visitors can also view the unique captive geyser that propels water more than 100 feet into the air.

Things to know

  • Every snowmobile operated on private or public land must be registered with the Idaho Department of Parks and Recreation on or before November 1 of each year.
  • Check the Southeast Idaho High Country website for details on trail maps, rental information, and lodging.
  • Cell reception may not be available--tell some where you plan to sled.
  • Full services are available are available in Soda Springs, Lava Hot Springs, Downey Malad City, Preston, and McCammon.
  • Dress for the conditions.
